Our Mission

We are on the mission to connect with inspring explorers arround the world and to motivate you regarding expeditions & advetnure travel. Expedition Connect provide you an easy and effective way to connect with explorers around the world. For emerging explorers and enthusiasts, it is a great a opportunity to closely follow and keep track of important activities around your favourite expeditions and experts. Additionally, you can join these experts in one of their field trips/tours or can take advantage of many workshops, trips, events, courses, volunteer programs, internships etc. organised by professional explorers.

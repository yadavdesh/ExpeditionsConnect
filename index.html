Our Mission

We are on the mission to connect with inspring explorers arround the world and to motivate you regarding expeditions & advetnure travel. Expedition Connect provide you an easy and effective way to connect with explorers around the world. For emerging explorers and enthusiasts, it is a great a opportunity to closely follow and keep track of important activities around your favourite expeditions and experts. Additionally, you can join these experts in one of their field trips/tours or can take advantage of many workshops, trips, events, courses, volunteer programs, internships etc. organised by professional explorers.

How it works?

Connect

Whether you are an expert or an enthusiast, connect with inspiring explores around the world.

Learn & Explore

Lean from experience or explore your favourite expedition together with your travel inspirations.

Inspire

"To be inspired is great and To inspire is incredible"

Get inspired with the great work and stories of explorers or inspire rest of the world by sharing your skills, expertise and experiences.

Explorers

Imagine observing lions on the prowl in the Serengeti with a wildlife biologist, examining ancient ruins in Peru with an archaeologist, or shooting images in Paris alongside a professional explorer. Now, our experts bring you to the planet's most intriguing places to share their knowledge and their insights. From photography to history to marine biology, our National Geographic experts are passionate about their fields, and they enrich the expedition experience through fascinating presentations and informal discussions.

Join the Revolution

We bring people with a passion for adventure, expedition, and life changing travel together

Have you always day dreamed of scuba diving off the coast of Micronesia? Or perhaps you’d love to trek through the jungles of Borneo, searching for rare birds and insects? Expeditions Connect is a social platform that introduces you to leading experts in exploration. Discover amazing adventures around the globe and learn from the most passionate people in the field.

Our experts are... Conservationists, Naturalists, Climbers, Explorers, Dive Masters, Marine biologists, Photographers, Authors, Historians, Archaeologists and more. With our global community of adventure experts there’s a world of exciting opportunities at your fingertips.

How it works?

Connect

Travel enthusiasts can find expeditions, activities, guides and experts based on real life feedback from other explorers. No travel agents or middlemen, just direct connection.

Explore & Learn

Whether it’s trekking in the Himalayas, scuba diving in the Seychelles, Expeditions Connect is where the adventure begins. Team up with professionals and experts who can guide you on your journey, both online and in real world expeditions.

Inspire

Get inspired by your fellow explorers around the world, and inspire others by sharing your stories, experiences and creative content.

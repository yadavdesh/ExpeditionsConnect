Explorers

Have you ever imagine observing lions on the prowl in the Serengeti with a wildlife biologist, examining ancient ruins in Peru with an archaeologist, or shooting images in Paris alongside a professional explorer? Our experts bring you to the planet's most intriguing places to share their knowledge and their insights. From photography to history to marine biology, our experts are passionate about their fields, and they enrich the expedition experience through fascinating presentations and informal discussions.
